Project Assignment

Preliminary selection

Students select one project from their list to focus on for their project this should include a formal investigation of the requirements of the project. If possible, identify similar applications that have been written and compare and contrast the objectives.

This application concept may be one or a combination of the concepts from the last assignment, or it may be something completely different.

The deliverable is a six to eight paragraph project proposal. This should contain a sufficient description of the project to allow construction of the project to begin.

 

Deliverable

Six to eight paragraph discussion of your proposed project. It should begin with a short introduction and conclude with a short conclusion. It should include discussions of the purpose of the software, an overall description of the high-level functional requirements of the software (that is, what the software will actually do), a survey of the relevant literature available that will assist you in completing your project, a short discussion of similar software, and a brief discussion of your project plan. Your writeup is not limited to two pages, and can include other sections that you feel will help the reader in understanding your proposal.

Remember that every system application has input, processing, and output. Identify what the process will be collecting (input) and what it will be producing (output).

Spend some time thinking about this. Your project will include creating a database and using an API.

  • What data will you store
  • What is the source for the data (upload? web scrape? external API?)
  • What are some ways the data could be used?

 

Your paper should have a title, author, and date. Use headings in your proposal. These should should have appropriate section and subsection headings if appropriate.
